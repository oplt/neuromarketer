## Research Goals

- Can the participant understand the Asset -> Goal -> Results flow without guidance?
- Can the participant choose a useful goal template, channel, and audience segment?
- Does the first visible result feel useful before final scoring completes?
- Where does the participant hesitate, backtrack, or ask for clarification?
- Which result sections drive confidence in the final decision?

## Task Script

1. Upload or select a creative asset.
2. Choose the campaign goal, channel, and target audience.
3. Start analysis and narrate what you expect to happen next.
4. Stop when the first useful result appears and describe what you learned.
5. Review the final result and decide whether to ship, iterate, or reject the asset.
6. Compare or export if that would be part of your real workflow.
